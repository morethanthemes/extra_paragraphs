Extra Paragraphs supplies a set of editor-friendly Paragraph types—like Highlight and Collapse—so content authors can compose modular, media-rich pages without custom development.

Why it helps

  • Pre-configured paragraph types for common patterns (highlight, collapse).
  • Editor and teaser displays for immediate preview.
  • Ready to combine with page builders and layouts.

Installation

composer require drupal/extra_paragraphs
# drush recipe extra_paragraphs
